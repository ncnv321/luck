About Me

Hello! You can call me luck, or Nico.

I'm 21, living in Toronto Canada.

Theres only so much I can type, but It's better to talk to me personally. Since my hiatus, I've met a lot of interesting friends and gotten back into my hobbies (art, IT, etc). Feel free to reach out if you'd like to work with me!

I work on a lot of passion projects like this, and experimenting with art mediums in my spare time and going to the gym and exploring and learning new things everyday.
